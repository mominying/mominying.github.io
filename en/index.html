The first season of DAN’S WAY-INDONESIA consists of ten episodes. It has been live on a Chinese video platform, Xigua Video, since November 18th, 2018 and made a big hit among both its audience and media. Up till now, the coverage of the related topic on social media has exceeded 300 million. In the following months, it will continue to be shown on, TV and other video platforms across China.
Now we are starting our voyage from Indonesia, with passion and expectations, curiosity and energy, in search of mind and soul, continuing sailing across the seas towards Brunei, Cambodia, the Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam.
We hope what you see and hear in the documentaries, the men and women, the laughter and tears, the music and dance, the soil and water, the past and present will make you feel proud of our people and proud of our region, a region of beauty and love.
Now join us in The Dan’s Way, a journey in praise of life.
